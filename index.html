Great Wall of China

  • The Great Wall stands today in 15 of China's current provinces
  • Over 5,500 miles of the Great Wall was built during the Ming Dynasty
  • The original purpose of the wall was as a boarder of defence
  • Large-scale battles were rarely fought at the Great Wall
  • Dispite common belief; the Great wall cannot be seen from space without the aid of technology
